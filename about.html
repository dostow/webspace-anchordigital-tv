Who We Are

Anchor Digital Media is a young dynamic company, with an innovative and fresh approach to Digital Media & TV Production. Our company works across a wide range of genres including but not limited to Drama, Entertainment, Reality Shows, Children’s Programme, Comedy, Factual, FeaturFilmses and Feature.

In addition to producing content for film and television, our company also focuses on visual effects production and motion graphics as essential parts of our business core.

What We Do

Our main area of focus and specialty is TV production however we also produce exceptional work in areas of Film and Digital Media. We have developed a 4-step process to TV production to simplify the creative cycle and allows us to be flexible in our service delivery to our clients, partners and sponsors.

  • Idea/concept Creation
  • Research
  • Production & Post-Production
  • Securing Airtime, License sales or Outright sales
